What is ecoSense?

Its object is to create a system for measuring the temperature in different positions of the domestic environment as well as measuring the electrical power in electronic devices. Through this system, a reduction in electrical energy consumption and an improvement in the quality of life in indoor environments is achieved.

Why us?

Temperature measurement

Through ecoSense, the temperature can be measured very simply. Unlike anyone else, we measure the temperature of bright positions of the house, to offer the most accurate measurement. All data accessible through graphics...

Power measurement

You can very simply measure the consumption of your electrical appliances through ecoSense. This way you can understand which of your devices consume a lot and thus save money.
